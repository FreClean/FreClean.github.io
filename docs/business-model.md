# Overview

FreClean operates a diversified cleaning solutions business model designed to combine professional cleaning services, high-quality cleaning products, entrepreneurship opportunities, and sustainable community development.

Our model focuses on creating long-term value for customers, partners, entrepreneurs, and communities through reliable services, scalable operations, and responsible growth.

---

# Business Model Vision

FreClean aims to build a sustainable ecosystem where:

- Customers receive professional cleaning solutions.
- Entrepreneurs access business opportunities.
- Communities benefit from economic development.
- Partners collaborate on shared growth.
- Sustainable practices improve environmental impact.
