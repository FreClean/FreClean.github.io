# Franchise Program

> **Version:** 1.0.0  
> **Last Updated:** July 19, 2026

---

# Overview

The FreClean Franchise Program is designed to expand professional cleaning services and cleaning product distribution through a network of trusted entrepreneurs and business partners.

The program allows qualified individuals and organizations to operate under the FreClean brand while benefiting from established systems, training, operational standards, and business support.

---

# Franchise Vision

FreClean aims to build a scalable franchise ecosystem that creates:

- Local business opportunities.
- Employment opportunities.
- Professional cleaning standards.
- Regional brand expansion.
- Sustainable entrepreneurship.

Our franchise model combines business growth with positive community impact.

---

# Franchise Mission

To empower entrepreneurs with the tools, knowledge, and support needed to build successful cleaning businesses while maintaining FreClean's standards of quality, professionalism, and customer satisfaction.

## Description

A business model focused on providing professional cleaning services using FreClean standards.

# 2. Product Distribution Franchise

## Description

A model focused on distributing FreClean cleaning products within a specific market area.

## Activities Include

- Local distribution.
- Customer relationships.
- Retail partnerships.

# 3. Combined Franchise Model

## Description

A complete FreClean business model combining:

- Cleaning services.
- Product distribution.
- Customer support.
- Local entrepreneurship.

This model creates a complete cleaning ecosystem.
