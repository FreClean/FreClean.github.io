# Product Safety

FreClean cleaning products must be managed responsibly.

Safety practices include:

- Clear product information.
- Proper labeling.
- Responsible storage.
- Appropriate handling instructions.
- Customer education.

Users should always follow product instructions and safety recommendations.

# Chemical Handling

Cleaning materials should be handled carefully.

Recommended practices include:

- Reading labels before use.
- Following manufacturer instructions.
- Using appropriate protective equipment when necessary.
- Avoiding unsafe mixing of cleaning substances.
- Keeping products away from unauthorized users.
- Storing products properly.

# Emergency Response

In case of a safety incident:

1. Stop the activity if possible.
2. Protect people from further risk.
3. Provide appropriate assistance.
4. Report the incident.
5. Review the cause.
6. Implement improvements.
