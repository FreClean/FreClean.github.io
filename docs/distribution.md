> **Version:** 1.0.0  
> **Last Updated:** July 19, 2026

# Overview

FreClean's distribution strategy is designed to make professional cleaning products and services accessible through an efficient, scalable, and customer-focused network.

Our goal is to build a reliable distribution ecosystem that connects products, services, entrepreneurs, businesses, and communities.

The distribution model supports sustainable growth by combining direct sales, local partnerships, entrepreneurship, and digital channels.

---

# Distribution Vision

FreClean aims to establish a trusted distribution network that enables:

- Easy access to quality cleaning solutions.
- Business opportunities for entrepreneurs.
- Strong relationships with customers.
- Regional market expansion.
- Sustainable supply chains.

FreClean's distribution system is guided by:
