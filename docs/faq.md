# General Questions

## What is FreClean?

FreClean is a professional cleaning initiative focused on providing high-quality cleaning products, professional cleaning services, entrepreneurship opportunities, and sustainable solutions for homes, businesses, and communities.

---

## What is the mission of FreClean?

FreClean's mission is to provide professional cleaning solutions and sustainable business opportunities that improve lives, strengthen communities, and create long-term value.

## Where does FreClean operate?

FreClean is designed for regional and international growth, with an initial focus on Caribbean communities and markets.

FreClean combines:

- Entrepreneurship opportunities.
- Community development.
