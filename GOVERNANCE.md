# Leadership

The leadership team is responsible for:

- Defining long-term strategy.
- Protecting the FreClean brand.
- Approving major initiatives.
- Managing organizational growth.
- Ensuring legal and ethical compliance.
- Maintaining financial sustainability.
- Overseeing operational performance.

Leadership decisions should always align with FreClean's mission and values.

---

# Decision-Making

FreClean follows a structured decision-making process.

## Operational Decisions

Routine operational decisions may be made by the responsible team or maintainer.

Examples include:

- Documentation updates
- Minor process improvements
- Routine maintenance
- Bug fixes
- Editorial corrections

---

## Strategic Decisions

Strategic decisions require leadership review.

Examples include:

- New business lines
- Major partnerships
- Brand repositioning
- Organizational restructuring
- Licensing changes
- Financial strategy
- International expansion

# Maintainers

Maintainers are responsible for:

- Reviewing pull requests.
- Managing releases.
- Protecting repository quality.
- Reviewing documentation.
- Enforcing project standards.
- Supporting contributors.
- Maintaining repository security.

Maintainers are expected to act professionally, fairly, and transparently.

---

# Contributors

Contributors are expected to:

- Follow the Code of Conduct.
- Respect project standards.
- Submit quality contributions.
- Write clear documentation.
- Communicate respectfully.
- Accept constructive feedback.
- Protect confidential information.

Every contribution should improve the project.

# Conflict Resolution

Disagreements should be resolved through respectful discussion.

When necessary:

1. Discuss the issue constructively.
2. Seek mediation from maintainers.
3. Escalate unresolved matters to leadership.
4. Document significant decisions.

Personal attacks and harassment are never acceptable.

---

# Code of Conduct Enforcement

All community members must comply with the Code of Conduct.

Maintainers may take appropriate action when violations occur, including:

- Private warning
- Public warning
- Temporary suspension
- Removal of content
- Restriction of repository access
- Permanent removal from community spaces

Serious violations may be reported to the appropriate platform administrators or legal authorities when required.

---

# Governance Review

This governance model will be reviewed periodically to ensure it remains effective as FreClean evolves.

Major governance updates should be documented through:

- Changelog entries
- Repository documentation
- Official announcements

---

# Policy Changes

Governance changes may be proposed by maintainers or leadership.

Substantial changes should include:

- A clear rationale
- Expected impact
- Implementation plan
- Review period

Approved changes become effective upon publication unless otherwise specified.
