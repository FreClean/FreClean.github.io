# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as members, contributors, maintainers, partners, and community leaders pledge to make participation in the FreClean community a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

We pledge to act and interact in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, diverse, inclusive, professional, and healthy community.

---

# Our Values

FreClean is built on the following principles:

- Respect
- Integrity
- Professionalism
- Collaboration
- Transparency
- Accountability
- Sustainability
- Continuous Learning
- Innovation
- Community Impact

Every interaction should reflect these values.

---

# Expected Behavior

Members of the FreClean community are expected to:

- Treat everyone with dignity and respect.
- Communicate professionally and constructively.
- Welcome different viewpoints and experiences.
- Accept constructive feedback gracefully.
- Focus on solving problems rather than creating conflict.
- Share knowledge openly when appropriate.
- Respect community decisions.
- Support new contributors.
- Report inappropriate behavior responsibly.
- Follow applicable laws and platform policies.

---

# Unacceptable Behavior

The following behaviors are not tolerated:

- Harassment of any kind.
- Discrimination.
- Hate speech.
- Personal attacks.
- Bullying or intimidation.
- Threats or violent language.
- Sexual harassment or unwanted advances.
- Trolling or intentionally disruptive behavior.
- Publishing private or confidential information without permission.
- Impersonation or identity misrepresentation.
- Spam or promotional abuse.
- Fraudulent or deceptive activities.
- Distribution of malware or malicious code.
- Any illegal activity.

---

# Professional Communication

When participating in discussions, issues, pull requests, or community spaces:

- Stay respectful.
- Stay on topic.
- Assume good intentions.
- Use clear and constructive language.
- Avoid inflammatory remarks.
- Be patient with newcomers.
- Resolve disagreements respectfully.

---

# Community Responsibilities

Community leaders and maintainers are responsible for:

- Enforcing this Code of Conduct fairly.
- Acting impartially.
- Protecting contributors.
- Encouraging constructive participation.
- Maintaining a welcoming environment.
- Addressing reported incidents promptly.

---

# Reporting Violations

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this Code of Conduct, report it privately to the FreClean maintainers.

Include, where possible:

- Description of the incident.
- Date and time.
- Relevant links or screenshots.
- Individuals involved.
- Any additional context.

Reports will be handled confidentially whenever possible.

---

# Enforcement

Community maintainers may take any action they deem appropriate, including:

- Private warning.
- Public warning.
- Content removal.
- Temporary suspension.
- Permanent removal from community spaces.
- Restriction of repository access.
- Reporting severe violations to platform administrators or appropriate authorities.

Enforcement decisions are made in good faith to protect the health and integrity of the community.

---

# Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all FreClean community spaces, including but not limited to:

- GitHub repositories
- GitHub Discussions
- GitHub Issues
- Pull Requests
- Documentation
- Social media operated by FreClean
- Community events
- Online meetings
- Public communications representing FreClean

It also applies when an individual officially represents FreClean in public or professional settings.
