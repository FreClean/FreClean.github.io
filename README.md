# About FreClean

FreClean is a professional cleaning company committed to delivering high-quality cleaning services, premium cleaning products, and innovative business solutions for homes, businesses, hotels, institutions, and communities.

More than a cleaning company, FreClean is building an ecosystem that combines professional services, product manufacturing, entrepreneurship, digital payments, and sustainable development.

By integrating modern technologies with environmentally responsible practices, FreClean aims to improve hygiene, create employment opportunities, and contribute to healthier communities throughout the Caribbean and beyond.

FreClean is proudly powered by the CeloHT ecosystem, leveraging Web3 technology to make business operations more efficient, transparent, and financially inclusive.
