# Becoming a Contributor

We welcome individuals who share our mission and values.

To become a contributor:

1. Read `README.md`.
2. Review `CONTRIBUTING.md`.
3. Follow the `CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md`.
4. Submit Issues or Pull Requests.
5. Participate in community discussions.

All contributions are reviewed according to project standards.
